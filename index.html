Every cake starts with a reason to celebrate

Creamyy Cakes & Desserts began as a home kitchen experiment and grew into Bhopal's go-to bakery for cakes that taste as good as they look. We hand-pipe every order, balance sweetness so it never overwhelms, and keep eggless versions of nearly everything on the menu — because a celebration shouldn't leave anyone out.

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

How much advance notice do you need?

Simple designs can often be done same-day if ordered before noon. Multi-tier fondant, wedding, and large bulk orders need at least 3–4 days' notice so we can plan the bake and design properly.

Do you make eggless cakes?

Yes — nearly every cake on our menu has an eggless version with no change in taste or texture. Just mention it when you place your enquiry.

Which areas in Bhopal do you deliver to?

We deliver across most of Bhopal including MP Nagar, Arera Colony, New Market, and surrounding areas. For locations further out, let us know and we'll confirm feasibility and charges.

Can I bring my own design or photo reference?

Of course. Share a reference photo, Pinterest board, or even a rough sketch over WhatsApp and we'll quote based on size, tiers, and detailing involved.

How do I pay?

We accept UPI, cards, and cash on pickup or delivery. For large or custom orders, a small advance secures your date.
